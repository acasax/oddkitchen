Privacy Policy (revised 10/2021)

Thank you for visiting The ODD Kitchens website, and we'd like you to know that we respect the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how The ODD Kitchen uses and discloses the personal information we collect from our website and/or mobile app.

This policy applies to the website, mobile app and any other websites operated by The ODD Kitchen. Your use of the website and/or mobile app constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy and your consent to the practices it describes.

The information The ODD Kitchen collects

The ODD Kitchen collects both personal and anonymous information from you when you visit our Website and/or send us information. The ODD Kitchen only obtains personally identifiable information such as your name and email address, and only other information that is voluntarily provided. We may collect information you share on either the mobile app or website and we may collect information about your food order.

The information will be used for the purposes for which you provide it. We may also use this information to communicate with you as to create personalized promotions by combining your personal information with non-personal information, such as the purchases you make or any benefits you receive through promotional programs.

How we may use your information

The ODD Kitchen may use information to process and fulfill online orders made directly through our mobile app, website or through a third-party delivery service or aggregator. The ODD Kitchen may share anonymous information for legitimate business purposes allowed under the applicable laws and regulations without consent or notification. The ODD Kitchen only shares personal information about a customer with third parties who are needed to fulfill your request for service, including vendors or other organizations. We may share some of your information with affiliated companies, in which case we will require these affiliates to provide the same degree of protection for your personal information as we do under this Privacy Policy.

The ODD Kitchen will not sell, transfer, or disclose your personal information to any third parties other than for the limited purposes described in this policy.

Protection of your information

The ODD Kitchen protects your information using technical security measures to reduce the risk of loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your information. Some of our safeguards include data encryption, firewalls, and administrative controls. If sensitive information (e.g. credit card information) is transmitted through our website or mobile app, the information is transmitted through a Secure Socket Layer (SSL).

Cookies and usage

We currently do not use cookies on our website. If in the future we decide to initiate the use of cookies, the following will explain its purposes. Cookies are used for a variety of purposes to improve and personalize your visits to our website or mobile app. Cookies are small pieces of data that a web server sends to be stored by your browser on your computer. Cookies allow us to recognize your computer or device while you are using our website or mobile app which helps us remember important information that will make your use of the website more favorable. Cookies help us track the total number of visitors to our websites on an anonymous aggregate basis. Cookies also enable us to remember you when you return to our website or mobile app. Options in your web browser can notify you when you receive a cookie or prevent a cookie from being sent. Please remember that if you disable cookies, some features may not be usable.

Links

Our website or mobile app may offer links to sites other than those maintained by The ODD Kitchen. These websites, including social media sites, have their own policies. If you visit one of these sites, you will subject to their policies. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Your rights

The ODD Kitchen respects your privacy and wants you to have control over how your personal information is used. Subject to local law, here are ways in which you can control your personal information:
• You may revoke your consent to the collection and use of your personal
information at any time.
• You can request us to erase or block the use of your personal information
• You can inform us of any changes to your personal information or have us correct any information we have about you
• The exercise of your rights is free of charge

Privacy policy updates

This Privacy Policy is subject to revision and is in effect on the date noted at the top of this page. If any changes have been made, we will post the updated policy on this webpage. Continued use of the Website following notice of any change shall indicate your acknowledgement of such changes and agreement to be bound by the revised terms of our Privacy Policy.
